Reconciliation Steps

  1. Count all cash notes in the drawer at the end of the day.
  2. Type the count into the Actual Closing Cash field.
  3. Verify the calculated Reconciliation Discrepancy.
  4. If there's a difference, detail the reason in the Notes.
  5. Click Save Reconciliation to lock in the sheet.
